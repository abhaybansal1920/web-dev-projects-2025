Characters of The Big Bang Theory

1. Sheldon Cooper

Sheldon is a theoretical physicist known for his genius-level intellect and quirky personality. He has a strict adherence to routines and often struggles with social interactions.

Quote: "Bazinga!"

2. Leonard Hofstadter

Leonard is an experimental physicist and Sheldon's best friend and roommate. He is more socially adept than Sheldon and often acts as a mediator in the group.

Quote: "Our babies will be smart and beautiful."

3. Penny

Penny is an aspiring actress from Nebraska who works as a waitress. She brings a different perspective to the group with her outgoing personality and street smarts.

Quote: "What up,moonpie?"

4. Howard Wolowitz

Howard is an aerospace engineer known for his flamboyant fashion sense and overconfidence with the ladies.

Quote: "I am a horny engineer."

5. Rajesh Koothrappali

Raj is an astrophysicist who initially struggles with selective mutism, making it difficult for him to speak to women unless he is under the influence of alcohol.

Quote: "You were so nice to me,I thought you liked me!"

6. Bernadette Rostenkowski-Wolowitz

Bernadette is a microbiologist with a high-pitched voice and a strong-willed personality. She is married to Howard.

Quote: "You mess with me I will break you."

7. Amy Farrah Fowler

Amy is a neurobiologist who becomes Sheldon's girlfriend. She shares many of his social awkwardness but is more emotionally expressive.

Quote: "All forms of physical contact,up to and including coitus,are off the table."
