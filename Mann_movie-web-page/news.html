KGF: Chapter 2 Release Date Announced

Fans of the KGF series can rejoice as the release date for "KGF: Chapter 2" has been officially announced. The much-anticipated sequel is set to hit theaters on July 16, 2021. Directed by Prashanth Neel, the film promises to deliver more action-packed sequences and an engaging storyline that continues the saga of Rocky.
